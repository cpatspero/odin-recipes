Cole's Cute Curry

Description

This is a wonderfully easy (and wonderfully tasting) curry dish that you can make in the comfort of your own home.

You only need a few ingredients and spices that you can find at most grocery stores near you.

Let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1lb Chicken Thighs
  • Low Fat Greek Yogurt
  • Large Yellow Onion
  • 6-7 Garlic Cloves
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garam Masala
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Tumeric
  • Cumin
  • 14oz Can Crushed Tomatoes
  • 400ml Coconut Milk
  • Knob of Butter
